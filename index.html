About

Myself

“What you think, you become. What you feel, you attract. What you imagine, you create.”

Buddha

My path as a seeker began, like many others, with a simple inquiry into meditation. What was it, and could it better my life? Led by my heart's innate desire to dive deeper and find meaning in the rapids of the river of life, following the questions rarely led me to more answers, but just more questions. It would seem, this is one of life's truths; yet during my search I found tools to inform my yearning.

The practices of yoga and reiki that have come to me through my own inquiries made a profound impact on my heart. And it has become clear that my path, my Dharma, is to share what I've learned with others. Through the practices of physical movement, meditation, and mantra, or some combination there of, we can all reconnect with our higher self and find solace and meaning in living presently, moment to moment.

Life is the guru to all of us, and if there is one lesson that it seems she keeps reiterating to me, it is that there is no mastery. I am a constant student, as are we all. All I can do is share what I've learned, and that is to lean into bliss as it shines itself upon your face.
